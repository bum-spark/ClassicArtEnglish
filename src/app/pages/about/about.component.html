The Concept

An E-Magazine About Classic Art

For this project, we chose "classic art" as the topic for an e-magazine that covers everything from ancient Greek ceramics to Baroque masterpieces, and even how AI and NFTs are reshaping the art world today.

The idea was to create something that feels like a digital art museum — where you walk through time, discover stories behind famous paintings, and understand how the same myths and themes have been reinterpreted across centuries.

The e-magazine format allowed us to combine visual storytelling with historical context, making art history accessible and engaging rather than just academic.

Justification

Why Classic Art?

Classic art represents a foundational chapter in human expression, ranging from ancient Greek sculpture to Renaissance masterpieces. We believe it is important because it connects us with ideas of beauty, storytelling, and identity that still shape modern culture.

By presenting this content in the form of a digital magazine, we aim to make art history accessible, visual, and meaningful — especially for audiences who may not have formal access to museums or academic art courses.
